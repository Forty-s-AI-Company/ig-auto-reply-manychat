# Impeccable Skill

- Use this after implementation to audit polish, hierarchy, spacing, and
  perceived completeness.
- Find controls that look interactive but do nothing.
- Find alerts, banners, and helper text that are technically correct but still
  confusing.
- Check empty states, loading states, and failure states, not just happy paths.

## InboxPilot-specific checks

- Sidebar account switcher clarity
- Inbox message pane balance and action affordances
- Contact detail editor consistency with contacts list
- Channel connect flows that may appear broken during OAuth or refresh actions
